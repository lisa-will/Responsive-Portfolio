About Me

I am a 28 year old Mother of one, beach loving baby. I fancy myself as an Artist, Writer, and Clean Water Activist. I hope one day to help push the envelope towards renewable energy sources and ensure clean water for future generations through web development.

Sound a little crazy? Here's some food for thought.

Speaking of food, I make a mean coconut curry! Some other fun facts about me are: I love languages, but am a master of none. I collect labbits, because they come in mystery boxes. I believe Sriracha or Tabasco sauce compliments every meal and may be known to carry a small travel-size bottle with me at all times.
I have lived on a hand-full of islands in the US including Manhattan, another is technically considered a peninsula, so don't let the name fool you. I have never been to California, but I have traveled to Poland, France, and London.

Can you guess the other islands? Here's a Hint: All are located on the East Coast of two polar opposite states.
